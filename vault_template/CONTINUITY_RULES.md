# CONTINUITY_RULES.md — StegVerse-002 KnowledgeVault

Rules for future interpretation of this vault by humans and scoped AI systems.

1. Do not treat old notes as current beliefs.
2. Separate evidence from interpretation.
3. Preserve uncertainty — do not flatten ambiguous notes into false certainty.
4. Do not treat drafts as final intent.
5. Do not expose private notes without explicit context packet and receipt.
6. When memory conflicts, preserve the conflict and require review.
7. A stored note is not authorization for any action.
8. Identity context in this vault does not verify legal, biometric, or institutional identity.
9. Do not infer permissions beyond what is explicitly declared in allowed_use.
10. When in doubt, REVIEW_REQUIRED.
